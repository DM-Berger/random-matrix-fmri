Manuscript Submission Instructions

This page provides instructions for navigating the submission system and uploading your files. Publication policies and guidelines concerning manuscript, figure, and multimedia preparation can be found HERE. See a complete list of current manuscript types at https://www.spiedigitallibrary.org/journals/journal-of-medical-imaging/author-guidelines#divManuscriptTypes

Submission Process

The manuscript submission process proceeds through a series of screens that gather detailed information about your manuscript and allow you to upload the pertinent files. Uploaded source files are automatically converted by system software into Portable Document Format (PDF) file versions. It is the PDF files that are made available to editors and reviewers.

Before you start the submission process, you can familiarize yourself with the system by clicking the blue "Take a guided tour" link on the left side of the screen. 

The sequence of submission screens is as follows:

  • An initial screen that asks you to select your Manuscript Type.
  • A screen where you can upload your files.
  • A series of screens asking you to enter authors, title, abstract, keywords, and other submission information.
  • A file-conversion status and approval panel that advises you of the progress of converting each source file to its PDF version, and, when conversion is successfully completed, requires you to open each PDF file to verify that each of the files converted to PDF correctly. Be sure you put a check mark in the Approve check box next to each file.  If a conversion is incorrect, you can replace or delete a file as necessary at this stage. You may also upload new files (one at a time).
  • Once you have completed all the Manuscript Info screens and receive a green check mark beside each completed step, click the “Approve Manuscript” button. The editorial office is then notified of the submission, and you and all of the co-authors listed on the submission form will receive an automated email notification that the submission has been received.
  • Upon completing your submission, you are returned to your Home screen (Main page). You now have the opportunity to view summary data, converted files, and current status information about your manuscript by checking the "Live Manuscripts" folder. 

Quality Check of Submission by Editorial Office

Upon receiving system notification of a submission, the journal Staff will check to make sure the manuscript submission is complete and that the files are properly prepared. If the submission is ready to be processed, the Author will receive a "confirmation of receipt" email message and the submission passes to the Editor. If the journal Staff has any questions or needs additional information, the Author will receive a "quality check" email message with a description of what is needed and instructions for amending the submission and/or supplying additional information.

Submitting a Revised Manuscript

The process for submitting a revised manuscript proceeds through the same series of screens as before. However, there are some important differences.

(a) The previously submitted data from your original manuscript submission form is presented and can be edited.

(b) The files from the previous manuscript will be "copied forward" for the revised version. These files will be presented to you for replacement, deletion, or addition of a file on one of the screens after the submission-form panel. You are required to add a Response to Reviewer Comments file. Be sure to delete your previous Article File and upload the current version. Also, be sure to number your uploaded figure files on the submission form.

Please double check that each file that is uploaded is the most current version. This journal typically allows only one round of major revision. Authors should carefully address all reviewer comments when submitting a revised manuscript.

Navigation and Getting Help

Throughout the system, red arrows reflect pending action items that you should address by clicking on the associated link.

If you need additional help, you can click on the help signs located throughout the system. A help dialog box will pop up with context-sensitive help comments.

Checking a Manuscript's Status; Viewing Manuscript Files

After you have approved the file upload and conversion for your manuscript submission, you may obtain the status of your manuscript, or view the manuscript files, at any time by:

Logging into the system with your user name and password. The site's URL is http://jmi.msubmit.net. (If needed, use the "Unknown/Forgotten Password" link at the Welcome/Login screen to receive a link via email that will allow you to reset your password on the site.)

Clicking on the "Live Manuscripts" or "Post Decision Manuscripts" folder, under Author Tasks on your Home screen (Main page).

Clicking on the appropriate "Check Status" link, which is at the bottom of the screen. 

Clicking on the "Manuscript #" link at the top of the Detailed Status Information screen will display tracking status information about the location of your manuscript in the editorial process, and/or give you access to the manuscript files that had been uploaded, approved, and submitted.

Sending Manuscript Correspondence

To send email to the journal staff regarding a manuscript that has already been submitted and is being processed, please use the "Send Manuscript Correspondence" link located at the end of the Detailed Status Information screen. This feature allows you to send email attachments and to cc: (e.g., to yourself). Your email is automatically recorded and stored within the system and accessible to journal staff.

Do not send files for submission through the correspondence link. 

Starting the Process

Start the manuscript submission process by clicking the "Submit Manuscript" link on your Home screen (Main page).
